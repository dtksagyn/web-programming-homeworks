Anne with an E

"Anne with an E", based on the novel by L.M. Montgomery, follows the imaginative, outspoken orphan Anne Shirley. Mistakenly sent to Green Gables, she wins hearts and confronts societal norms with her bold mind and sensitive soul.

About the Series

Set in 19th-century Prince Edward Island, this coming-of-age drama explores identity, belonging, love, and the power of imagination. It celebrates resilience, friendship, and the transformative power of acceptance.

Main Characters

Anne Shirley-Cuthbert

The Imaginative Orphan

She has boundless imagination, red hair, and a strong personality. She can express even the deepest emotions with words.

Marilla Cuthbert

The Stern Guardian

Practical and strict at first, but slowly opens her heart to Anne. Beneath the surface lies deep love and care.

Matthew Cuthbert

The Gentle Soul

Shy and soft-spoken, but with immense kindness. Matthew forms a strong, tender bond with Anne from the very start.

Gilbert Blythe

The Charming Rival

Smart, competitive, and kind-hearted. His relationship with Anne evolves from rivalry to deep affection.

Diana Barry

The Loyal Best Friend

Elegant and kind, Diana is Anne's most trusted friend. She embraces Anne's bold spirit with grace and warmth.

Cole Mackenzie

The Sensitive Artist

Talented and introspective, Cole finds confidence and friendship through Anne’s understanding and encouragement.

Sebastian Lacroix (Bash)

The Wise Friend

Compassionate and grounded, Bash provides Gilbert with support and brings maturity and heart to every scene he’s in.

Jerry Baynard

The Loyal Farmhand

Hardworking and sincere, Jerry becomes part of the Green Gables family with his honesty and quiet strength.
